# Coffee Shop Sales & Customer Behavior Analysis
There is a Coffee Shop Dataset from April 2019 across three NYC outlets. The dataset includes representative retail data from April 2019 across three outlets in New York City. It encompasses thousands of transactions, various product categories (such as coffee beans, pastries, and beverages), staff details, customer information, inventory, sales targets, and more. This project examines the Coffee Shop Sample Dataset, which covers sales receipts, product details, customer demographics, and operational metrics from a single month in 2019.

The analysis aims to uncover insights into top-performing categories, outlet and staff performance, customer behavior, temporal sales trends, retention rates, average order values, inventory efficiency, target achievement, promotional effectiveness, and a focused funnel analysis on coffee beans.

**3. Identified Business Problems, Insights, and Impact**

• **Top-selling products:** Identify best-sellers to optimize inventory, marketing, and revenue growth.

• **Outlet/staff performance:** Recognize high performers to improve management and incentives programs.

• **Sales trends:** Track daily, weekly, and hourly peaks for forecasting and planning.

• **Customer segments:** Segment demographics to tailor marketing and boost repeat purchases.

• **Retention vs acquisition:** Balance loyal customers and new acquisitions for sustainable growth.

• **Average transaction value:** Track AOV to uncover upselling and pricing opportunities.

• **Inventory waste impact:** Reduce overproduction to improve efficiency and profit margins.

• **Sales target achievement:** Monitor targets to identify outlets needing operational support.

• **Promotion effectiveness:** Evaluate campaigns to design smarter, higher ROI marketing strategies.

• **Coffee Bean types:** Identify high-performing types to align production and marketing.

• **Coffee Bean products loyalty:** Pinpoint repeat-purchase items for tailored loyalty programs.

• **Price/promotion impact:** Analyze factors driving purchases to refine strategies.

• **LTV and RFM profile:** Quantify and segment high-value Coffee Bean customers.

• **Generational spend frequency:** Understand habits to optimize pricing, packaging, and product sizes.

• **Staff Coffee Bean sales:** Link staff performance to product success and rewards.

• **Customer segment repeats:** Segment demographics to improve retention and lifetime value.



**4. Conclusion**

This analysis provides essential insights into sales patterns, customer behavior, and operational efficiency. It highlights leading categories, top staff and outlets, and key customer segments while pinpointing opportunities to improve inventory management, promotions, and retention. Using these data-driven insights, the coffee shop can optimize operations, enhance customer satisfaction, and support sustainable growth.
